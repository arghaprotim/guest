ABOUT CALL OF WILD WINGS

NATURE, a simple, six letter word, is the utmost of everything. For us, Nature is the ultimate GOD, like a Mother, who exists, is front of youreye. We believe that, as Nature gives us all the necessary things andessentials, since we born, it is our duty to return her somethinganddo something better for her. So, we, Call of Wild Wings have plannedseveral things for the betterment of nature through explore, discover and conserve her elements as much as possible. We, Call of Wild Wings not only organize customized or groupbased Birding Trips / Wildlife Trips in different parts of India, (Specially in Northern West Bengal, Sikkim and Ladakh)withour professionals, but also, guide nature enthusiasts, amateurs andchildren, who are the future pillars of conserving Nature. Wilderness, Trekking, Birding is in our vein, and which is more than anythinginour life. We believe, that we born for Wild. We started our journeyat 2017, till 2023 we finished 60plus successful trips.
